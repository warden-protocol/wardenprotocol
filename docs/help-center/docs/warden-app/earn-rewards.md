# Earn rewards

## Overview

In this section, you'll discover how to start **earning rewards** in Warden:

1. [Create a coin](#create-your-coin): Create your own coin.
2. [Earn reWARD points](#earn-reward-points): Stay active in Warden to boost your coin's rank.
3. [Get rewards](#get-rewards): The top reWARD holders will be rewarded.

## Create your coin

To create **your own coin**, take these steps:

1. Log in: 👉 [Warden](https://app.wardenprotocol.org)
2. In the left menu, open the **Rewards** tab.
3. Then simply name your coin.

Congratulations! Now you can start earning reWARD points.

![Create and grow your coin in Warden](../../static/img/warden-app/earn-rewards-1.png)

## Earn reWARD points

### Check your rank

**reWARD points** are your reward for activity in Warden. The more reWARDs you earn, the higher your coin climbs on the leaderboard.

The top reWARD holders will [be rewarded](#get-rewards).

To check your coin's rank, open the **Rewards** tab and scroll down to the **reWARD Leaderboard**:

![View the reWARD leaderboard in Warden](../../static/img/warden-app/earn-rewards-2.png)

### Complete tasks

To get reWARD, **complete tasks** in Warden:

- Swap
- Use AI Agents
- Play in-app games
- And more

We're continuously expanding the task list with new ways to earn.

To access all available tasks and see the reWARD points you can earn, simply go to the **Rewards** tab and scroll down:

![Complete tasks in Warden to earn rewards](../../static/img/warden-app/earn-rewards-3.png)

### Tap to reWARD

A fun way to collect more reWARD is by playing the **Tap to reWARD** game:

- Each tap costs **$0.10**. To learn more, see [Tap to reWARD fee](fees#tap-to-reward-fee).
- Every tap grants you **10 reWARDs**.
- Every 10 taps, you unlock a chance to hit a reward boost: **x10**, **x15**, or **x20**.

You can access the game from any tab—simply click **Tap to reWARD** in the left menu:

![Play the Tap to reWARD game in Warden](../../static/img/warden-app/earn-rewards-4.png)

### Trade

To earn additional reWARDa, join **League of Traders** and climb its leaderboards.

You can either participate in perpetual trading or play our trading game:

- [Trade](trade#earn-rewards)
- [Play Betflix](play-betflix#earn-rewards)

## Get rewards

The most active Warden users will gain priority access to upcoming features and other rewards.

Keep [earning reWARDs](#earn-reward-points) and stay tuned for updates!
