# Stake $WARD

## Overview

[$WARD](https://docs.wardenprotocol.org/ward/introduction) is the native token of Warden Protocol. If you hold $WARD, you can **stake** it to participate in the network's security and earn **rewards**. The more you stake, the more rewards you receive!

This section explains how to stake $WARD, claim rewards, stake more, or unstake.

:::note
When staking, you **delegate** your tokens to a preferred **validator** acting on your behalf. To learn more, see [Warden validators](#warden-validators).
:::

## Warden validators

**Validators** are nodes that participate in the consensus process to secure the Warden Protocol network. They validate transactions an produce new blocks. By staking, you **delegate** your $WARD to a validator to earn a share of the rewards.

Learn more: [How to choose a validator?](https://docs.wardenprotocol.org/operate-a-node/delegation-plan#how-to-choose-a-validator)
